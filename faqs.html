Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Can I request a refund if I am not satisfied with the product?

Of course! Our simple returns policy gives you the peace of mind that you can request a refund by contacting us via our call center manager.

How can I contact customer service?

You can contact us for any request by filling out the form available on this page. We will be happy to answer your query.

How long will it take for my products to be delivered?

Delivery is made via express courier service, and you will receive the product at your home within 24 to 48 hours after placing your order, excluding holidays.

Can I pay cash on delivery?

Yes, this payment method is preferred by most of our customers. By choosing to pay in cash only after receiving the product, you can make your purchase with complete security.

Where can I buy the product?
