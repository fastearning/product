Refund Policy

1. Introduction

At Qolor Co., we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not satisfied with the product you have received, we offer a simple and straightforward refund policy.

2. Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund, the following conditions must be met:

  1. The product must be unused and in the same condition that you received it.
  2. The product must be in the original packaging.
  3. You must contact us within 30 days of receiving the product.

3. Refund Process

If you meet the eligibility criteria, you can initiate a refund by contacting our customer service team. Please follow these steps:

  1. Fill out the refund request form available on our website.
  2. Provide the order number and reason for the refund request.
  3. Once we receive your refund request, we will review it and notify you of the status.

4. Refund Timeline

Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after we receive the returned product. The refund will be credited back to your original payment method. Please note that delivery charges are non-refundable.

5. Non-Refundable Items

Unfortunately, some items are non-refundable, including:

  • Products that have been opened or used.
  • Items that are on sale or discounted (unless defective).
  • Gift cards and digital products.
